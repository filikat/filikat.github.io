Filippo Zanetti

I'm a Postdoc in the School of Maths at the University of Edinburgh, currently collaborating with the HiGHS team.



Research interests

My main fields of research are numerical linear algebra and numerical methods, in particular applied to constrained optimization. My work often involves iterative Krylov solvers, preconditioners and interior point methods.

I am currently developing a factorization-based interior point methods for the HiGHS software library.

I have worked also with numerical PDEs, finite elements methods, multigrid preconditioners, low-rank updates, eigenvalue solvers.

I am interested in the application of these concepts to real life problems like imaging, optimal transport, sparse approximations, machine learning...
